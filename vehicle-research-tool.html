Editor's Pick
If you only read one entry

At $7,500, the strongest single pick across every gas category is a well-maintained 2014 Acura TSX with under 130k miles. For trucks: 2010–2012 Honda Ridgeline RTL. For SUVs: a rust-free 2007–2010 Toyota 4Runner V6.

For electrics, the rules are different. Avoid air-cooled Leafs in Phoenix. If you can stretch to $13–15k, a 2017–2019 Chevy Bolt with a documented battery replacement is the single best used EV value in America — new pack, larger capacity, fresh 8-year warranty.
