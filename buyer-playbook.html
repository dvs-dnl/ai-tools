Before You Go
Do this at home before the visit
~15 min
⚠ Try to see the car cold. Call ahead and ask the seller not to warm it up first. A warm engine hides cold-start knocks, rough idle, and startup smoke that reveal bad head gaskets, worn rings, and misfires. If you arrive and it's already warm, note it.
2
Exterior — Body & Paint
Stage 1 of 5 — Walk the car before touching anything
~8 min
The panel gap test. Crouch at each corner and sight along the car's body. Factory panels have perfectly even gaps. Mismatched gaps between hood/fender, door/door, trunk/quarter-panel = past collision repair. Pair this with paint texture differences and paint "witness marks" on hinge bolts.
